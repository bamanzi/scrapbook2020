Star Trek: Year Five IDW ongoing series

A new continuing series about the end of the Five-Year Mission! The classic crew is back in all-new stories! The Star Trek: Year Five showrunners are Jackson Lanzing and Collin Kelly.

#365 Feb 2019
Previews


Contains solicits for April 2019 and features a Star Trek: Year Five front cover. The cover art by Greg Hildebrandt was also used for a promotional poster given away at WonderCon 2019.

Cover artist: Greg Hildebrandt

#1 Apr 2019
Star Trek: Year Five
Solicitation: The crew of the U.S.S. Enterprise left Earth four years ago. They've traveled to strange new worlds, defeated impossible foes, and made universe-changing decisions. But now, with the end in sight, they'll have to face their biggest challenge yet. Step aboard the Enterprise with Kirk, Spock, Bones, Uhura, Sulu, Scotty, and Chekov as they begin the end of their original Five Year Mission and boldly go into an uncertain future in this new continuing Star Trek series!

There will be multiple retailer incentive covers for the debut issue.

Writers: Jackson Lanzing, Collin Kelly
Artists: Stephen Thompson, Charlie Kirchoff
Cover artist: Greg Hildebrandt
