Gotham (TV Series)
First Aired Last Aired
September 22, 2014 April 25, 2019

Gotham is a live action television series that started in the fall of 2014 on Fox, about Jim Gordon as a rookie cop in Gotham City after the murders of Martha and Thomas Wayne. Produced by The Mentalist and Rome creator Bruno Heller and Judge Dredd director Danny Cannon, the series focuses on the heroes and villains before they donned their iconic costumes.
