Cacowards

Since 2004, the year following 10 years of Doom, Doomworld has marked Doom's birthday by hosting an annual award ceremony honoring the year's top releases. Continuing the 10 Years' format, a handful of the best wads are chosen and discussed in detail, including the best multiplayer map, best comedy map, longest development time, and the mapper of the year, which honors a prolific developer in the community. Anyone looking for a collection of the greatest projects created for Doom should read through the following articles, as well as 5 Years of Doom and 10 Years of Doom.
