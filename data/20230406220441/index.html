The Eighth Doctor’s Timeline

Here is ‘The Eighth Doctor’s Timeline’.

I’ve referred to

  • ‘The History of the Doctor’ website – http://www.doctorwhoreviews.altervista.org/Index.html

and

  • The Eyespider ‘Doctor Who’ site index – http://eyespider.org.uk/drwho/compleat.html

to help me put this version of the timeline together.

*Thanks to Constonks and elGranto on ‘The Divergent Universe’ forum for their insight help and knowledge on the Eighth Doctor; comics and short trips in ‘Doctor Who’.

I’ve included all the TV stories, audios; books; graphic novels/comics; short trips and the Puffin e-book ‘Spore’ into this list for now.

The (TV) and (Audio) stories are highlighted in ORANGE.

The (Book) stories are to remain un-highlighted for now.

The short stories are highlighted in ORANGE and/or ITALICS; and are CENTRED.

The stories with links to reviews by me on my blog are highlighted in BOLD and ORANGE.

Regarding the (Book) stories, I’ve identified each of them as follows in the KEY below.

KEY
*VNA – Virgin New Adventures
*EDA – Eighth Doctor Adventures
*PDA – Past Doctor Adventures
*TB – Target Books

Regarding the (Audio) stories, I’ve identified each of them as follows in the KEY below.

KEY
**BAB – BBC Audio Book
**BFAD – Big Finish Audio Drama
**BFAB – Big Finish Audio Book
**PAB – Puffin Audio Book

Here is a list of abbreviations for some of the (Audio) stories and what they mean.

  • (DRS) – ‘The Diary of River Song’

Regarding the short stories, I’ve identified each of them as follows with abbreviations.

  • (AIL) – Adventures In Lockdown
  • (ST) – Short Trip (either Big Finish or BBC)
  • (SS) – ‘The Scientific Secrets of Doctor Who’
  • (TS) – Target Storybook
  • (TWP) – ‘The Wintertime Paradox’
  • (PT) – Perfect Timing
  • (PT2) – Perfect Timing 2
