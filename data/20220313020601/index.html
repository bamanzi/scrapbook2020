DracoDruid wrote:Does someone still has the 1st and 2nd season and could reshare them?


I checked the sources for various episodes from both seasons, and they were all fully sourced, plus the incomplete, plus all the sources through KAD.

However, when you add a whole season to your client, you are (logically) standing in queue to the same person or persons.
There is a limit to how many files you are able to stand in queue for from a source (so that other people has a chance getting some files too).
This means some episodes might appear to be without a source at all, because, as the client says, you have already "asked for another file".

To solve this issue you can:
1) ignore it and go do something else
2) pause some files
3) download only a few episodes (or even one) at a time
